WHAT WE TREAT

⠇
Wound Care
⠇
Pressure Ulcers
Skin breakdown from prolonged pressure; common in low-mobility patients.
⠇
Diabetic Ulcers
Foot & leg wounds from poor circulation due to diabetes.
⠇
Venous Ulcers
Open sores from blood pooling in the veins; usually on the lower legs.
⠇
Arterial Ulcers
Reduced blood flow to the extremities — feet, toes, and outer ankles.
⠇
Infected Wounds
Bacterial contamination blocking natural healing; requires targeted treatment.
⠇
Chronic & Post-Surgical Wounds
Non-healing wounds >4 weeks or post-surgical wounds past 90 days.
⠇
Burns & Traumatic Wounds
Heat, chemical, or trauma injuries needing specialized protocols.
