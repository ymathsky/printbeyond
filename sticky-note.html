VistaPrint Upload Guide — Custom Notepad / Sticky Notes

  • Click ⬇ Export Hi-Res (600 DPI) to download the PNG at full print quality.
  • On VistaPrint, search for "Custom Notepads" → choose 3×4 inch, 50 or 100 sheets.
  • Upload the downloaded PNG as your design. Select Glossy coated finish for vibrant color.
  • The red hatched border on-screen is the bleed guide — it will NOT appear in the export or on print.
  • Proof carefully: keep the company name fully within the safe (non-hatched) area.
