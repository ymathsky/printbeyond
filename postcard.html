VistaPrint Upload Instructions

  • Click ⬇ Export FRONT (Hi-Res) and ⬇ Export BACK (Hi-Res) to download both PNG files at ~600 DPI.
  • VistaPrint product: Standard Postcard — 4″ × 6″ (landscape).
  • Upload front.png as the Front side and back.png as the Back side.
  • The red dashed border shown on screen is the bleed guide (⅛″ per side) — it does NOT print. Keep all critical content inside the safe area.
  • VistaPrint will auto-crop to the bleed line, so the exported PNG edges may be trimmed by ~26px per side.
  • For best results, choose Glossy finish in VistaPrint for vibrant teal/accent colors.
